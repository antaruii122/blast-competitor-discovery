# Findings

## Research
- **Goal:** Technical parity engine for global hardware vs LATAM market.
- **Key Categories:** Cases, Monitors, Water Cooling, Keyboards, Mice, Gaming Tables.
- **Target Brands:**
    - High End: MSI, Cooler Master, Corsair, Be Quiet!
    - Mid/Entry: Redragon, Antec.

## Discoveries
- **Input Source:** Google Sheets (requires AI parsing to extract standardized specs).
- **Validation:** Strict +/- 15% numeric variance for match validity.
- **Gatekeeping:** Missing features = automatic disqualification.

## Constraints
- **Geography:** South America (Chile, Brazil, Colombia).
- **Data Persistence:** Supabase `Matches_Mercado` table.
